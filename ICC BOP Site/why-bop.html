Hidden Costs

What nobody shows you
in the demo.

Every competitor has costs that don't appear in their marketing. These are the numbers field service owners hit 90–180 days in — after the contract is signed.

💳
Payment Processing Fees

Jobber, HousecallPro, and others charge 2.9% + $0.30 per credit card transaction on top of their monthly fee. On a $50,000 month of invoicing — over $1,450 in processing fees alone, every month, on top of software costs.

BOP: Use your own payment processor. No in-platform transaction fees.
📞
Phone System Add-Ons

Workiz's phone system is a separate add-on. ServiceTitan's communication tools are tiered above base. For a dispatching operation, integrated calling and SMS is a basic requirement — sold back to you as an upgrade.

BOP: Communication integrations configured at setup. No add-on required.
🔒
Contract Lock-In & Exit Penalties

ServiceTitan requires 12–36 month minimum contracts. Documented early exit penalties range from $10,000 to $40,000+. You find out what leaving costs only after you're trying to leave.

BOP: 6-month minimum, then 30 days notice. Your data is yours and exportable.
🧩
The Safety Software Nobody Includes

Every competitor on this page requires a separate safety documentation tool for WCB compliance — approximately $394 CAD/mo for a 10-person field team. Not mentioned on any of their pricing pages.

BOP: Safety documentation hub included in every tier. No separate tool needed.
⏱️
Crew Time Tracking Is Extra

Jobber and HousecallPro don't include crew time entry. Most teams end up buying a separate time tracking tool at ~$120 CAD/mo — then manually reconciling it with their scheduling software. Data entry you're paying for twice.

BOP: Crew time entry linked directly to jobs. Flows into job costing automatically.
🎓
Implementation and Training

With Jobber and HousecallPro you configure and train yourself. With ServiceTitan you pay $7,000–$49,000 and still spend months in onboarding. Either way the cost lands on you — in money, time, or both.

BOP: Full implementation and team training included in setup fee. Live in 2 weeks.
